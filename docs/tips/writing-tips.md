# Resume Writing Tips

Craft compelling content that gets results.

## General Principles

### Be Specific

**Weak:** "Improved sales"

**Strong:** "Increased quarterly sales by 23% through targeted email campaigns"

### Use Action Verbs

Start bullets with strong verbs:

| Instead of | Use |
|------------|-----|
| Responsible for | Led, Managed, Directed |
| Helped with | Supported, Contributed, Collaborated |
| Worked on | Developed, Built, Created |
| Did | Executed, Implemented, Delivered |

### Quantify Everything

Numbers catch attention:
- Revenue generated
- Team size managed
- Projects completed
- Percentage improvements
- Time/money saved

## Section-Specific Tips

### Summary

**Length:** 2-4 sentences

**Include:**
1. Your professional identity
2. Years of experience (if impressive)
3. Key strength or specialty
4. What you're looking for (optional)

### Experience

**Format each bullet:**
- Action verb + task + result/impact

**Example progression:**
- "Managed social media accounts" ← OK
- "Managed social media accounts, growing followers 150%" ← Better
- "Grew social media followers 150% in 6 months through data-driven content strategy" ← Best

### Skills

**Organization tips:**
- Group by category (Technical, Soft Skills, Tools)
- Put most relevant first
- Include skill levels if meaningful
- Remove obvious skills (Microsoft Word, email)

### Education

**For recent graduates:**
- Include GPA if above 3.5
- List relevant coursework
- Highlight academic achievements

**For experienced professionals:**
- Degree and school are usually enough
- Remove graduation year if it dates you

## Common Mistakes

### Too Much
- Paragraphs instead of bullets
- Every job duty listed
- Irrelevant experience
- More than 2 pages (for most roles)

### Too Little
- Missing metrics
- Vague descriptions
- No context for achievements
- Skills without evidence

### Formatting Issues
- Inconsistent formatting
- Typos and errors
- Hard-to-scan layout
- Too many fonts/styles

## Writing Process

### Step 1: Brain Dump
Write everything you did in each role, without editing.

### Step 2: Select
Choose the most impressive and relevant points.

### Step 3: Strengthen
Add metrics and results to each point.

### Step 4: Polish
Refine language, check consistency, proofread.

## Tailoring Tips

For each application:
1. **Mirror language** — Use words from the posting
2. **Reorder bullets** — Most relevant first
3. **Adjust emphasis** — Highlight what they want
4. **Check alignment** — Does your resume answer their needs?

## Final Checklist

Before submitting:
- [ ] No typos or grammatical errors
- [ ] Consistent formatting throughout
- [ ] Contact info is current
- [ ] Action verbs start each bullet
- [ ] Numbers and metrics included
- [ ] Tailored to this specific role
- [ ] 1-2 pages in length
- [ ] Easy to scan in 10 seconds
