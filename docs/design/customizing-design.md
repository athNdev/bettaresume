# Customizing Your Design

Access design settings from the **"Design"** section in the left panel.

## Colors

### Primary Color
- Used for headers, accents, and links
- Choose something professional but distinctive
- Consider matching the company's brand (subtly)

### Secondary Color
- Used for subtitles and secondary text
- Usually a neutral gray works well

### Tips
- Blues and dark grays are universally professional
- Avoid overly bright or neon colors
- Ensure sufficient contrast for readability

## Typography

### Font Family
Choose from professional fonts:
- **Inter** — Modern, highly readable
- **Georgia** — Classic serif
- **Roboto** — Clean and neutral

### Font Size
- Adjust base size for readability
- Larger fonts if you have less content
- Smaller fonts to fit more on one page

### Heading Style
- Bold, underlined, or colored
- Consistent throughout the document

## Layout

### Margins
- Adjust page margins (0.5" to 1")
- Smaller margins = more content space
- Larger margins = cleaner look

### Section Spacing
- Compact for longer resumes
- Spacious for shorter resumes

### Line Height
- Affects readability
- 1.2-1.5 is typical

## Pro Tips

- Preview on different screens before exporting
- Print a test page to check formatting
- Dark mode in the app doesn't affect export
- PDF always exports in light mode
